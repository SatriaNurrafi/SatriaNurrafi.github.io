How to make Tuna Pasta? November, 17 2021

Ingredients:

  1. Pasta 100g
  2. 1 liter water
  3. Canned tuna
  4. 3 cloves garlic
  5. Chilli
  6. Salt
  7. Oil

Tools:

  1. 2 pans
  2. Spatula
  3. Knife
  4. Cutting board
  5. Plate
  6. Tongs

Steps:

  1. First, Heat the water until it boils, then add the pasta and wait until it’s cooked.
  2. Second, Heat the oil in a pan
  3. Then, Chop 3 cloves of garlic.
  4. Slice the chilies thinly.
  5. After that Saute garlic, chili and tuna in a pan.
  6. Put the cooked pasta into the pan.
  7. Stir and add salt then wait a while.
  8. Next Put the finished pasta on a plate.
  9. Finally the tuna pasta is ready
